Privacy Policy

TFMP Field Agent · Last updated: 15/05/2026

This Privacy Policy explains how MEEM FACILITY MANAGEMENT ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the TFMP Field Agent mobile application (the "App"). The App is an internal tool provided to authorized personnel for conducting and recording field inspections.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information when you use the App:

  • Account information. When you sign in, we collect your account credentials and associated identifiers (such as your name, work email address, and user role) used to authenticate you and associate your activity with your account.
  • Location information. With your permission, the App accesses your device's location — including while the App is running in the background — to record where inspections are carried out and to support inspection workflows in the field.
  • Photos and camera. With your permission, the App uses your device's camera and photo library so you can capture or attach photographs as evidence for inspections.
  • Inspection data. Information you enter or generate while using the App — including inspection records, notes, assessments, timestamps, and associated media — which is transmitted to and stored on our backend systems.
  • Device and usage information. Basic technical information such as device model, operating system version, and app version, used to operate, secure, and troubleshoot the App.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and provide access to the App.
  • To record, process, and store field inspection data.
  • To associate inspections with the correct location, personnel, and time.
  • To operate, maintain, secure, and improve the App.
  • To comply with applicable legal and regulatory obligations.

3. How Your Information Is Stored and Protected

Inspection data and account information are transmitted to and stored on backend systems operated by or on behalf of MEEM FACILITY MANAGEMENT. We use reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to protect information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

The App is an internal business tool. We do not sell your information, and we do not use third-party advertising or analytics services in the App. We may share information only:

  • Within MEEM FACILITY MANAGEMENT and with authorized personnel who need it to carry out inspection-related operations.
  • With service providers who host or support our backend systems, under appropriate confidentiality obligations.
  • Where required by law, regulation, legal process, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of MEEM FACILITY MANAGEMENT or others.

5. Data Retention

We retain account and inspection information for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this Policy, to meet operational and recordkeeping needs, and to comply with applicable legal and regulatory requirements.

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • You can control the App's access to location, camera, and photos at any time through your device's system settings. Disabling these permissions may limit App functionality.
  • Because the App is provided in connection with your role at MEEM FACILITY MANAGEMENT, requests to access, correct, or delete account or inspection information should be directed to us using the contact details below and may be subject to internal policy and legal requirements.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is intended solely for use by authorized personnel in a professional capacity. It is not directed to, or intended for use by, children, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.
